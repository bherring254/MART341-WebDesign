My Mother Told Me

'My Mother' is a popular and old Chonobi Lausavísa. Chonobi Lausavísa is poetry of a single stanza composition, or a set of stanzas unconnected by narrative or thematic continuity. While having no official author or publish date, it has been passed down for generations. It is usually sung during traveling or drinking. 



My mother told me
Someday I would buy
Galley with good oars
Sail to distant shores
Stand up on the prow
Noble barque I steer
Steady course for the haven
Hew many foe-men, hew many foe-men﻿
      

Þat mælti mín móðir,
at mér skyldi kaupa
fley ok fagrar árar,
fara á brott með víkingum,
standa upp í stafni,
stýra dýrum knerri,
halda svá til hafnar
höggva mann ok annan.
